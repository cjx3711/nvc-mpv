How it works

Snap

Just take a picture of your pastries like you would normally do. Don't worry, the app will prompt you to share if you forget.

Share

Share the picture along with a discount code with all your friends on social media. The more the better.

Get Rewarded

When anyone uses your discount code, you get points. Simple as that. You can use these points to redeem all sorts of rewards.
