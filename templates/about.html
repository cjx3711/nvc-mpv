More About Our Company.

We're just a bunch of students passionate about pastries. We want to share this passion with the rest of the world, we believe that by creating this platform, we will bring joy to many more people.

The company was created around the idea that there must be some way to reduce food wastage. By providing discounts and advertising to expiring foods, we hope to reduce the amount of food that gets thrown away daily.
